Jazzy Winter Nights

Hillary Clinton and other V.I.P.s. attended a Jan. 25 benefit concert at Jazz at Lincoln Center to support cancer research at <pos>Columbia University Medical Center</pos>. A pediatrics benefit for NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and <pos>Weill Cornell Medical College</pos> was held Jan. 19 at the Mandarin Oriental. The 63rd Winter Antiques Show had an opening night party on Jan. 19 to benefit the East Side Settlement House. And the Metropolitan Museum of Art hosted a new winter party on Jan. 23 to support diversity and inclusion.
